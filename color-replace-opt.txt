The yellow replacements:

Mocha: Yellow → Teal (#94e2d5) for a cool cyan-green vibe
Macchiato: Yellow → Lavender (#b7bdf8) for a soft purple tone

Your custom themes will start first in the rotation, so you can immediately see the no-yellow versions! The original Catppuccin themes are still there if you want to compare.
Want to tweak the replacement colors? Just change the values in the ansi[4] and brights[4] positions (those are the yellow slots). You could try:

#cba6f7 for a stronger purple (mauve)
#a6e3a1 for green
#89b4fa for blue
